Understanding PDF to Image Conversion

Portable Document Format (PDF) is great for documents, but sometimes you need an image format like JPG or PNG to share on social media or embed in a website. This tool rasterizes the vector data of a PDF into pixel-based images, making them easy to view and share without a PDF reader.
