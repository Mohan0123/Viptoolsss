How to Use the Discount Calculator

Our Discount Calculator helps you quickly figure out how much you will actually pay at the register. Whether you are shopping for clothes, electronics, or groceries, knowing the final price after a percentage off is essential.

  1. Enter Original Price: Input the price tag amount of the item.
  2. Enter Discount: Input the percentage discount (e.g., 20 for 20% off).
  3. Optional Tax: If your location charges sales tax or GST, enter the percentage to get an exact total.
  4. Calculate: Click the button to see your savings and final price instantly.

How Discounts Work

A discount is a reduction in the basic price of goods or services. It is usually expressed as a percentage. The formula used by our tool is:

Savings = Original Price × (Discount Rate / 100)
Final Price = Original Price - Savings

If you are also calculating tax, the tax is usually applied to the discounted price, not the original price, which saves you even more money. Use our GST Calculator for more specific tax breakdowns.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

1. What is 20% off of 100?

20% off of 100 is 20. The final price would be 80.

2. How do I calculate a discount manually?

Convert the percentage to a decimal (e.g., 25% becomes 0.25). Multiply the original price by this decimal. Subtract that result from the original price.

3. Does this tool store my data?

No. All calculations happen in your browser. We respect your privacy.
