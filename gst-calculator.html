How to Use the GST Calculator

The VIP Tool GST Calculator is designed for both business owners and consumers. It allows you to perform two main functions:

  1. Add GST (Exclusive): Use this when you have a net amount and need to calculate the final bill including tax. Enter the amount, select the rate, and click "+ Add GST".
  2. Remove GST (Inclusive): Use this when you have a total bill amount and want to find out the original price before tax. Enter the total amount, select the rate, and click "- Remove GST".

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

1. What is the formula for adding GST?

To add GST: GST Amount = (Original Cost x GST Rate) / 100.
Then, Total Amount = Original Cost + GST Amount.

2. What is the formula for removing GST?

To remove GST (Inclusive): Original Cost = Total Amount / (1 + (GST Rate / 100)).
Then, GST Amount = Total Amount - Original Cost.

3. How is GST split in India?

For intra-state sales (within the same state), the GST is split equally between CGST (Central) and SGST (State). For inter-state sales, it is charged as IGST (Integrated).
