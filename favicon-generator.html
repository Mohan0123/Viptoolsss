Why Your Website Needs a Favicon

A favicon is more than just a small icon; it's a key part of your brand identity. Here is why you should use our Favicon Generator:

  • Brand Recognition: Helps users identify your tab among dozens of open browser tabs.
  • Professional Look: A site without a favicon shows a generic globe icon, which looks unfinished and untrustworthy.

Technical Details & Formats

Historically, favicons used the .ico format. However, modern web standards fully support PNG format for favicons. Our tool generates optimized PNG files.

  • 16x16: The classic size for browser tabs.
  • 32x32: Used for taskbars and high-DPI (Retina) screens.
  • 180x180: The standard for iPhone and iPad home screens.
  • 192x192 & 512x512: Essential for Android Chrome and Progressive Web Apps (PWA) manifest files.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

1. Do I need to convert PNG to ICO?

For modern browsers (Chrome, Firefox, Edge, Safari), PNG is perfectly acceptable. You can simply use <link rel="icon" type="image/png" href="favicon.png">.

2. What is the best image size to upload?

We recommend uploading a high-quality square image, at least 512x512 pixels, to ensure the generated icons are crisp and clear.
